Privacy Policy

Coffee Shop Privacy Policy

At our cozy coffee shop, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Below is an outline of the information we collect and how it is used:

Information We Collect

When you visit our coffee shop, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and payment details for processing purchases and improving our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to process orders, communicate with you about your purchases, and enhance your overall experience at our coffee shop. Your personal information is never shared with third parties.

Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Our website is securely encrypted to ensure that your data is protected. Additionally, we have internal policies in place to safeguard your information.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not store any personal information and are used for analytical purposes only.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected on this page, so we encourage you to check back periodically.
